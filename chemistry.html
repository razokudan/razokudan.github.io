Chemistry

Let me try to give a summary of all that I am supposed to know. Go to Wikipedia for more information (or fact checking):

Chemistry is the study of the transformations of substances. Substances can be single elements (like H HE LI BE B C N O ...) or compounds (like H2O C2O CH4, O2 (Oxygen) is not a compound but a diatomic element).

Mixtures

Mixing substances is part of physics, as the substances do not combine to transform into a new substance. Nonetheless mixtures are part of chemical experiments.

The three forms of mixtures:

Solutions

A solution consists of at least one solvent in which the solutes are getting diluted into. Thus the substance with the greatest amount in the solution usually is the solvent.

A homogeneous solution has one phase, i.e. it appears uniformly.

A heterogeneous solution has at least two phases, i.e. at least one solute e.g., settles as a sediment.
